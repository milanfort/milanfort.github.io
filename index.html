About

I'm Milan Fort, a full-stack software engineer and aspiring data scientist living in Frankfurt, Germany. My main professional interests include reactive programming, modern web development, data analysis, and search engines. For front-end web development, I prefer to use vanilla JavaScript (the good parts). On the backend, I develop primarily for the JVM, where Scala is my language of choice. When analyzing data, I consider R to be the most powerful tool in my toolbox.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from P. J. Safarik University in Kosice, Slovakia, and a Master's degree in Software Systems Engineering from RWTH Aachen University, Germany.

Please see my resume for more details about my education and work experience. Feel free to contact me if you want to get in touch.
